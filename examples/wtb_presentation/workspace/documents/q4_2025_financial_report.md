# Q4 2025 Financial Report - TechFlow Inc.

## Executive Summary

TechFlow Inc. reported strong Q4 2025 results, with total revenue of $142.3 million, representing a 23% year-over-year increase. Operating income reached $28.4 million, up from $19.2 million in Q4 2024.

## Key Financial Highlights

### Revenue Breakdown

| Segment | Q4 2025 | Q4 2024 | YoY Growth |
|---------|---------|---------|------------|
| Cloud Services | $78.5M | $58.2M | +34.9% |
| Enterprise Software | $45.2M | $42.1M | +7.4% |
| Professional Services | $18.6M | $15.4M | +20.8% |
| **Total** | **$142.3M** | **$115.7M** | **+23.0%** |

### Profitability Metrics

- **Gross Margin**: 68.2% (up from 65.4% in Q4 2024)
- **Operating Margin**: 20.0% (up from 16.6% in Q4 2024)
- **Net Income**: $21.8 million
- **EPS (Diluted)**: $1.42

### Cash Flow and Balance Sheet

- Operating Cash Flow: $35.2 million
- Free Cash Flow: $28.7 million
- Cash and Equivalents: $245.8 million
- Total Debt: $125.0 million
- Net Cash Position: $120.8 million

## Segment Performance

### Cloud Services Division

The Cloud Services division continues to be our primary growth engine. Key developments:

- Active enterprise customers increased to 2,450 (up 28% YoY)
- Average revenue per customer: $32,000 annually
- Customer retention rate: 94.2%
- New product launches contributed $12.3M to Q4 revenue

### Enterprise Software Division

Enterprise Software showed steady growth with improved margins:

- License revenue: $28.4M
- Maintenance revenue: $16.8M
- New customer acquisitions: 127
- Average deal size increased 15% to $245,000

### Professional Services Division

Professional Services expanded its consulting practice:

- Billable utilization: 78%
- Average billing rate: $185/hour
- Project backlog: $42.5M
- Customer satisfaction score: 4.6/5.0

## Regional Performance

| Region | Revenue | % of Total | YoY Growth |

## 2026 Outlook

Management provides the following guidance for FY 2026:

- **Revenue**: $620-640 million (18-22% growth)
- **Operating Margin**: 21-23%
- **Cloud Services Growth**: 30-35%
- **CapEx**: $45-50 million

### Strategic Priorities

1. Accelerate cloud platform adoption
2. Expand international presence, particularly in APAC
3. Invest in AI/ML capabilities
4. Pursue strategic acquisitions in adjacent markets

## Risk Factors

- Macroeconomic uncertainty affecting enterprise IT spending
- Competitive pressure from larger cloud providers
- Currency fluctuations impacting international revenue
- Talent acquisition and retention challenges

## Conclusion

Q4 2025 demonstrated TechFlow's ability to execute on its growth strategy while improving profitability. The company remains well-positioned for continued expansion in 2026.
